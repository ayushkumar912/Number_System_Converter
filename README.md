# Number_System_Converter

Conversion algorithms: The software should implement the following conversion algorithms for positive integers:
Excess-3 to decimal ,
Decimal to excess-3 ,
Excess-3 to binary ,
Binary to excess-3 ,
Excess-3 to octal ,
Octal to excess-3 ,
Excess-3 to gray ,
Gray to excess-3 ,
Decimal to binary ,
Binary to decimal ,
Decimal to octal ,
Octal to decimal ,
Binary to gray and 
Gray to binary
